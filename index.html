Dear Sir/Madam,

This notice formally records the cumulative impact of client-issued drawing revisions and change orders on JSSL's Design Office and production operations for the above project, from January 2025 to date.

1. DRAWING RELEASE DELAY - CURRENT
Structural drawings for June 2026 production phases (Indent 887) were required to be available by 1 March 2026 to allow JSSL to raise MTOs and POs within the 90-day 'drawing received-to-steel delivered' window. For 360.6 MT of the June plan, drawings were either not received or received after this window had closed. As a direct consequence, no RM has been ordered for these phases and JSSL's June production plan is short by 360.6 MT with no recovery path within the month.

2. DESIGN OFFICE REWORK - CUMULATIVE BURDEN
Since January 2025, DLF has issued 9 formal Engineering Change Notices (ECNs) and 15 additional informal change events against this project. The cumulative impact on JSSL's Design Office:

3. ASSEMBLY HOLDS
As of 15 May 2026, 1,202 steel members are on assembly hold across DT8 and MOIG zones. Of these, 635 members were in active production when the hold was placed - meaning labour had already been expended before DLF's revision was received. JSSL requests return approvals on all outstanding held items by 22 May 2026.

4. DELETED STEEL
286 members (123.1 MT) were fully fabricated before DLF removed them from the structural design entirely - comprising 169 members (78.7 MT) from the Assembly Hold Register and 117 members (44.3 MT) from VF005 Annexure III, all confirmed fabrication-completed prior to deletion.

JSSL reserves all rights in connection with the above and will raise formal variation claims for recoverable costs in accordance with the contract. This notice serves as a formal record that DLF has been made aware of these cumulative impacts.
